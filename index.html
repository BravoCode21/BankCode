Welcome

Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank (ADIB), is a fully operational bank institution that has combined the best of traditional, ADIB values technology and innovation that characterize the best of modern banking. Backed by its age-old values of being Progressive, Innovative, Ethical and Reliable, ADIB Bank has put in place channel capabilities, high quality of service and a sound business model to ensure that it is ‘The Optimal Way to Bank’.

About Us

In line with our mission to provide Islamic banking solutions to the global community, we pursue an ambitious growth strategy. Our presence in the UAE has been remarkable. We already have operations in Egypt, Sudan, the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, the United Kingdom, Iraq, among many other global countries. In addition, we are currently extending our operations to several markets across the Middle East and beyond. Iraq is a vital part of our international growth strategy

Initiatives

In line with our continued efforts to give back to the society, as ADIB we believe we have a corporate social responsibility of aiding our country and the society at large. As an organization, we have been involved in supporting and sponsoring various initiatives such as UAE genetic disease association, Emirates Association of the Visually Impaired, Beit Al Khair Society among so many other noble initiatives
